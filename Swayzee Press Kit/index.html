BIOGRAPHY

Ezekiel Georgewill Swayzee professionally known as “SWAYZEE” is a musician from Rivers State, Nigeria, born in Lagos on the 17th of March, 1998. He was raised in Portharcourt into a family that was not musically inclined.
Swayzee started music professionally in 2018, he claims that he got the motivation from a woman whom he was in love with and in no time he was signed into Etins record on the same year.


He had no major interests in taking music professionally, it was just street rap battles, vibes and cyphers. His rap battle alias was “FATAL EMCEE”.

Swayzee is a fan of astronomy, spirituality, magic video games and his hair. He places a lot of importance on his hair, it’s his favorite and most important part of his body. Talking about his love for spirituality, magic and astronomy, we can see that his logo and aesthetics revolves round this extraterrestrial concept.

One of his favourite quotes is

They do it for the likes, I do it cause I like- swayzee

Going through phases, his major inspirations had varied overtime from Mr Eazi and Runtown down to Burna Boy and Wizkid, his favourite from all time and globally remains Tory Lanez. Come 2020, Swayzee started making Audiomack exclusive freestyles. This started from his mini fanbase on Facebook which has now grown rapidly as has evolved into a community called “Shhtars”. Even though his songs were unofficial releases, they did very well.

Over 500k+ listens on spotify

His first official single DMS was released 9th of April, 2020 under Etins Records. DMS revealed another side of the SWAVE we never thought existed, it was something out of the ordinary. Showcased more of his vocal capabilities unlike the regular which was calm and mostly low keyed. DMS currently has 500k+ plus streams across all platforms and the numbers keep running up everyday.
